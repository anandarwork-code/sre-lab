- multi_health_check.sh — SSHes into all 3 VMs, checks 5 critical services with color-coded output
  - Monitors: nginx, sreapp (sre-vm1), mysqld (sre-db), prometheus (sre-mon), gitea (sre-vm1)
  - Runs every 15 minutes via cron — logs to ~/sre-lab/logs/multi_health_check_log.txt
  - Uses reusable check_service() function with local variables and positional arguments
- mysql_backup.sh — automated mysqldump, timestamped, 7-day cleanup, credentials in /root/.my.cnf
